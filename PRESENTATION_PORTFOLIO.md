- Background: Clean white or light gray
- Text: Dark blue/black for contrast

**Font Recommendations:**
- Headlines: Bold, modern sans-serif (Poppins, Montserrat)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Inter, Roboto)
- Code/Technical: Monospace (Fira Code, Courier New)

**Visual Elements per Slide:**
- **Slides 1-3:** Minimal, text-heavy, professional

- **Slide 13:** Charts, skill bars, visual progress indicators
- **Slide 14:** Infographics, numbers highlighted, badges
- **Slide 15:** Inspirational image, forward-looking visuals

**Animation Suggestions:**
- Titles: Fade in
- Bullet points: Appear one by one
- Stats: Number counters (0 → final number)
- Images: Zoom in or fade
- Transitions: Subtle (no more than 0.5s)
